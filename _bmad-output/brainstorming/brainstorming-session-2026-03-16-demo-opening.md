# Brainstorming Session Results

**Facilitator:** Snnair
**Date:** 2026-03-16

## Session Overview

**Topic:** How should the NC demo open? Biology, message, or the question the programme answers?
**Goals:** Generate diverse opening approaches for the 3-minute Encode x Pillar VC fellowship demo voiceover — find the opening that lands simultaneously with ARIA science reviewers and VC partners in the first 10 seconds

### Session Setup

Fresh session. Context: NC — NeuroAI Cognitive Companion. Encode x Pillar VC AI for Science Fellowship submission. Demo must answer "how do you work?" in under 3 minutes. Two audiences simultaneously: ARIA neuroscience reviewers (want rigour) and Pillar VC partners (want the hook). The opening ten seconds sets the contract with the viewer — what kind of experience this will be, what they should care about, and why they shouldn't look away.

### Compass Answers (from Snnair)

**1. The one feeling after 3 minutes:**
"Give this project a chance — don't pass it off as science-flavoured product. The hypothesis is that it can save humanity and requires rewiring the human brain."

**2. Voiceover:** Yes — Snnair's voice. Adds credibility. Builder speaking.

**3. The truest line written:**
"Human productivity is at low scale due to high emotions, ego play, misunderstanding. If we can overcome this we can achieve much more. There is untapped potential waiting to be explored."

### Facilitator Analysis

That third answer is the real opening. It's not about biology. It's not about messages. It's about **the civilisational cost of miscommunication** — the productivity, creativity, and connection that never happens because the amygdala fires before the PFC understands. That's the wound. That's the stakes. That's the reason this matters beyond neuroscience.

The feeling Snnair wants — "give this a chance" — tells us the opening must *disarm scepticism before it forms*. Not by proving scientific rigour immediately. By making the problem so undeniable, so personally felt, that dismissal becomes impossible.

## Round 1 — Question Storming

### Questions the opening must answer:

**For ARIA reviewer:** Is this scientist or builder? What scientific gap? Done before? Falsifiable?
**For VC:** Why does this matter to me personally? How big? Why this person? What's the advantage?
**For the human watching:** Have I felt this? Is this about me? Why hasn't this existed? Is this serious?

**Questions the opening must NOT trigger:**
- "Oh, this is just Grammarly with neuroscience branding."
- "Interesting concept, but not real science."
- "Too ambitious. Too vague. Next."

**Key insight:** Lead with untapped potential (loss framing), not wound framing. Everyone knows the wound. Almost nobody has named the scale of what's being lost.

## Candidate Openings

### Opening A — The Untapped Potential
*Yellow Hat + Untapped Oil Metaphor*

> "The largest untapped source of human potential isn't a new model, a better algorithm, or a smarter tool. It's the gap between what humans mean — and what other humans receive. High emotions, ego, misunderstanding — these aren't personality failures. They're biology. A forty-millisecond subcortical pathway that fires before meaning is understood. This project builds the first scientific instrument to measure that gap, intervene on it, and test whether the human brain can be rewired away from it. That's not a product pitch. That's a hypothesis. And this is the evidence."

**Why it works:** Opens with scale, grounds in Snnair's truest line, lands science in sentence three. Both audiences engaged. No scepticism triggered.

---

### Opening B — The Naming
*Green Hat + First Telescope Metaphor*

> "There's a number that exists for every human relationship — and no one has ever been able to see it. It's the distance between what one person means and what the other person receives. We call it the intent gap. It's computable. It's measurable. And — we hypothesise — it's changeable at the neural level. Before this instrument, it was invisible. This is the instrument."

**Why it works:** "We call it" is a founding gesture. Reviewers feel they're witnessing a discovery. Precise. Confident. Not overselling.

---

### Opening C — The Cost
*Red Hat + Diagnosis Metaphor*

> "Think about how much human productivity, creativity, and connection never happens — not because of lack of effort or intelligence, but because a message landed wrong. Multiply that by three billion messages a day. That cost has a biological mechanism. And for the first time, we have an instrument to measure it, an intervention to address it, and a scientific programme to test whether the brain itself can change. This is that programme."

**Why it works:** Opens with civilisational cost (Snnair's core belief), scales immediately, pivots to science. Emotional setup earns the rigour that follows.

## ✅ Full Voiceover Script (Final)

### OPENING — before screen comes alive (~15 sec)

> *"The largest untapped source of human potential isn't a new model or a better algorithm. It's the gap between what humans mean — and what other humans receive. We call it the intent gap. It has a biological mechanism: a forty-millisecond subcortical pathway that fires before meaning is understood. High emotions, ego, misunderstanding — these aren't personality failures. They're biology. And they're costing us more than we can measure. This project builds the first scientific instrument to measure that gap, intervene on it, and test whether the human brain can be rewired away from it. Here's how it works."*

---

### DEMO BEATS — screen recording with sparse narration (~90 sec)

**Beat 1 — The measurement:**
> *"Same message. Different relationship. Watch the intent distribution shift. The gap is computable. This is what we're training a model to measure."*

**Beat 2 — The intervention:**
> *"Before she hits send — the system fires. Not a correction. A signal. The cognitive reappraisal window. Forty milliseconds, reclaimed."*

**Beat 3 — The arc:**
> *"Over sixty days, we hypothesise the brain rewires. TPJ strengthens. Threat-first becomes meaning-first. That's not a product claim. That's H₃ — and that's what the EEG pilot is designed to test."*

---

### CLOSING ARC — vision frames (~20 sec)

> *"In twelve months: a validated dataset, a fine-tuned model, a 60-day EEG pilot, and a beta cohort of real relationships generating real gap scores. But the endpoint isn't a model. It's not an app. The final goal is a trust-based network — starting with two individuals who choose to make themselves legible to each other, then expanding outward. As AI absorbs more of the cognitive labour of human communication, what remains irreducibly human is the quality of connection between people. This is the instrument that makes that quality measurable — and improvable — at scale. That's what AI for Science is built for."*
