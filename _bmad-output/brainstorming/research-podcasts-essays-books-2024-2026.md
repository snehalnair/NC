## Essays and Blog Posts

**Dario Amodei — "Machines of Loving Grace" (Oct 2024)**
- darioamodei.com
- 50-page essay on "what if everything goes right." Biology, neuroscience, economics, meaning
- Themes: (7) symbiosis, (2) stratification
- NC relation: Optimistic vision NC exists within. Conditional on risks being managed

**Mario Brcic — "The Memory Wars: Cognitive Sovereignty" (arXiv, Aug 2025)**
- arxiv.org/abs/2508.05867
- Coined "Cognitive Sovereignty." AI memory creates lock-in. Proposes memory portability
- Themes: (1) cognitive atrophy, (2) stratification, (6) alignment paradox
- NC relation: THE most directly relevant paper. Academic framework for NC

**Xu et al. — "Cognitive Agency Surrender" (arXiv, Mar 2026)**
- arxiv.org/abs/2603.21735
- "Zero-friction" AI creates automation bias. Proposes "Scaffolded Cognitive Friction"
- Themes: (1) cognitive atrophy, (5) skill obsolescence, (7) symbiosis
- NC relation: Strongly supports. Technical framework for augmentation AI design

**"Cognitive Castes" (arXiv, 2025)**
- arxiv.org/html/2507.14218v1
- AI creates epistemic stratification — cognitive class divisions
- Themes: (2) stratification, (4) truth collapse
- NC relation: Strongly supports democratizing cognitive augmentation

**CIGI — "The Silent Erosion" (2025)**
- cigionline.org
- ChatGPT users: 47% drop in neural engagement, 80% couldn't recall what they'd written
- Themes: (1) cognitive atrophy, (5) skill obsolescence
- NC relation: Empirical backing for cognitive atrophy problem

**Cartik Hosanagar — "AI is Deskilling You" (Substack, 2025)**
